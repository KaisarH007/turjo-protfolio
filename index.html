My Projects

Smart IoT Biofloc System (On going for conference)

In this project 4 sensor (pH,TDS,NH3,Temperature) has been used to monitoring all the parameters of water. The values shared in server through firebase and a 20*4 LCD is for real time monitoring. From firebase the values shows in a android app. The app consist of 5 parameter (pH,TDS,EC,Temperature,NH3) and 3 switches (Heater ,Pump, Servo). The servo has been used for make the sensor up & down in water to take reading from them and other 2 switches to activate heater and supply water to make balance in water parameters.

Rubik's Cube Solver Robot

Hardware: Arduino,MG996R Metal Gear Servos and Mechanical Push, Bucket and Hold hand. Via Python by using a GUI,6 Side’s Colors of the cube input. The GUI solve the cube by Kociemba algorithm and send signals to the arduino to produce the PWM for the Servos to solve the Rubik’s Cube.

Face Mask Detection Based Automatic Gate

“Face Mask Detection Based Automatic Gate” is an embedded system that works based on google teachable machine.It is a machine learning online tool for everyone. By providing some image samples can make the tool understand two deferent types of pictures by a simple training and make a model link. In this project ESP32 Camera Module used which is connected to the internet through it’s built in Wifi. Attach the model link in ESP32 Cam and take some images and analyse at teachable machine for make a decision. After making decision it informs the Arduino to open the gate and show some indication related to gate on LCD and LED.

Face Recognition & Temperature Detection Based Automatic Gate

Here esp32 Cam has been used to detect face and recognize faces moreover an arduino in used for to take reading from MLX90614 IR temperature sensor. When sensor reading in between safe limit and face also familiar then the servos open gate.

Automatic Bottle Filling System

Hardwares: IR sensor,Gear Motor,Water pump,Arduino ,Relay ,LCD Push Button. Push Buttons are used to set the pump on time and start stop and digit selection. After Putting the bottle on the conveyor when the sensor detect conveyor stops and pump starts to flow water in the bottle and wait a moment for the drops of water and start running conveyor and stops again in front of another sensor to take the bottle from conveyor.

Campus Automation

Hardwares: Arduino Pro mini, Ultrasonic Sensor, Blue tooth Module, 4-Channel Relay Module,Servo motors, MLX-90614 sensor,MQ-2 Smoke sensor,Buzzer,20*4 I2C LCD,PIR sensor,Pump, IR Sensor, Switching Circuit.This is a special project for covid situation.It is divided into 5 portions. No.1 It’s senses the temperature of human from distance without any touch by MLX-90614 and if the temperature is normal(Less than 98.6° F) then it opens the gate of campus otherwise it makes sound and do not open the gate. No.2 For Sanitization a touchless activated water tap implemented by IR sensor, Switching circuit and pump. No.3 A touchless dustbin it detect human hand by ultrasonic sensor and open/close the cover with the help of metal gear servo. No.4 Voice Control class room automation by android app> Bluetooth >Arduino>Relay Module>Loads. No.5 Smoke Detection by MQ-2 and make beep sound when smoke/gas Detected.

Bend Product Check Tester

Hardware :2N3904 BJT ,Resistors,Diode,Fibre Sensor,Limit Switch.Some of our product found bended in japan. So I designed a tester which can detect minimum 1.2mm bend of the product. I Designed this circuitry only using BJT and implemented NO,NC action. When there is not have any product on tester then Yellow Lamp is ON,When put the product at the tester then, yellow lamp become off and decision is given by the tester by the Green lamp or Red lamp and Buzzer.
